There are plenty of different terms used to describe property campaigns, but there are really only two ways of marketing - private treaty and auction. Choosing the right one for you is easy.

If you...

...aren’t in a
hurry and don’t
want to feel any
time pressure.

...want
to dissuade
inspections from
buyers who have
searched in a
different price
bracket.

...don’t
mind the
prospect of
opening your home
for more inspections
over a longer
period.

If you...

...are keen to
compress the time
it takes to finalise a
sale.

...want to attract
all possible buyers,
regardless of their
original budget.

...are willing to
create time
pressure for buyers
to increase
competition.

Then choose to market your property via private treaty.

Then choose to market your property via auction.
